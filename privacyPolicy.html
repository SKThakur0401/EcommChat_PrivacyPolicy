Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Customer Support Chat Application ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of its users. This app is intended for internal business communication between companies, clients, and employees.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, Aadhar card number (for employee registration), and profile photo.
  • Account Credentials: Login email and password (handled via Firebase Authentication).
  • Media Files: Shared images, videos, PDFs, and documents are uploaded via Firebase Storage.
  • Device Information: We collect device tokens for push notifications using Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and enable secure login.
  • To allow real-time chat and group calling between clients and employees.
  • To provide role-based features to the admin (account creation, monitoring, notifications).
  • To send push notifications about new messages, group calls, or admin broadcasts.
  • To analyze communication patterns and enhance service quality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All user data is securely stored in Google Firebase services and is only accessible to authenticated users based on their roles (admin, client, or employee).

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you are blocked by the admin, your data remains secure and can be restored if unblocked. You may request account deletion by contacting us at saumya.thakur.dev@gmail.com.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13 years of age.

6. Data Security

We use Google Firebase’s secure infrastructure for authentication, data storage, and file transfers. All communications are encrypted using HTTPS and Firebase’s secure APIs.

7. User Rights

  • You can request to access, update, or delete your data.
  • You can ask the admin to deactivate or remove your account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. All changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
