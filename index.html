Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Ecomm Chat ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects user data. This application is intended for internal communication between businesses, employees, and clients.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, Aadhar (for employees), and profile photo.
  • Authentication: Email and password via Firebase Authentication.
  • Media & Files: Images, videos, PDFs, and documents via Firebase Storage.
  • Device Info: Device tokens for push notifications using Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify and authenticate users via secure login.
  • To facilitate real-time chat, media sharing, and group calling.
  • To enable admins to manage roles, monitor activity, and send broadcasts.
  • To send real-time push notifications (messages, calls, system alerts).
  • To maintain and improve app functionality and communication experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All user data is stored securely in Google Firebase services and is only accessible to authorized users based on their roles (admin, employee, client).

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If your account is blocked by an admin, data remains stored securely and can be reactivated later. Account deletion is possible by user request.
